Krystal's Chipotle Chicken

This is a delicious Chipotle Chicken marinated to perfection by Krystal. Get ready to dive into a burst of flavors with this simple and easy recipe!

Arguably even better than the original restaurant favorite! The chicken can sit in the marinade overnight for an easy weeknight dinner. Cook the chicken on the stove top, in the oven, on the grill, or in the air fryer, and then use it in your favorite meal

Ingredients

  • Chicken Thighs
  • 1 Yellow Lemon
  • Small Handful of Cilantro
  • 3 Garlic Cloves
  • Chipotle Peppers with Adobo Sauce, 1 for mild, 2 for spicy
  • Large pinch of Salt
  • Large pinch of Pepper
  • 2 Large pinches of Oregeno
  • 1 Tablespoon of Olive Oil

Directions

Step 1

In a blender, open one can of chipotle peppers and place 1 pepper into the blender and about a few tablespoons of the adobo sauce

Step 2

Following up, go ahead and add juice of 1 Lemon, and 1 Tablespoon of Olive Oil

Step 3

You may now add the Garlic Cloves, followed up by all the seasoning (Salt, Pepper, Oregano)

Step 4

Last but not least, add about a small handful of Cilantro (Including the Stems)

Step 5

Blend all the ingredients to achieve your marinade (Add a little bit of water if needed)

Step 6

Pour the Marinade over your Chicken Thighs and let it marinade for about 30 minutes to a few hours. If no time to let set, it is still enjoyable as is!

Step 7

Cook thighs flipping every 10 minutes for 30-40 minutes

Enjoy :)

Nutrition Facts

Calories: 400

Fat: 10g

Protein: 20g

Carbs: 22g
